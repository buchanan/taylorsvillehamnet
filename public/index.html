Net Information

Mondays @ 2030 hrs local time
146.740(-) Tone 114.8  or 448.100 (-) Tone 114.8


​Our net is held on a linked repeater system located on the Huntsman Cancer Center Hospital
so you can use either frequency and be heard.

Our back up repeater will now
be the 146.940 (-) Tone 88.5
