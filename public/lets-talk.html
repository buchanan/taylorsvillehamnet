IS YOUR HAM RADIO EQUIPMENT 
JUST SITTING THERE?    
              LET'S TALK!


If you are a licensed HAM radio operator living in or near the Taylorsville, Utah area, we invite you to become involved in the TAYLORSVILLE HAMnet.


We welcome your talents and expertise in our efforts to provide the community with dedicated radio operators, community event radio support and emergency communication services.  


If you want to become a licensed HAM radio operator, contact us for information on how you can do that.

Contact us @ tville.hamnet@gmail.com     or 


Use the "Contact us" tab to leave us your thoughts and/or questions.  


Return back to the "Home" page to get information on our net.
